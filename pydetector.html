property auto_comp_disable¶

[Jungfrau] Enable or disable auto comparator disable mode.

Note

By default, the on-chip gain switching is active during the entire exposure. This mode disables the on-chip gain switching comparator automatically after 93.75% of exposure time (only for longer than 100us).

Default is 0 or this mode disabled (comparator enabled throughout). 1 enables mode. 0 disables mode.
